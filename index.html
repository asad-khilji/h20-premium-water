What We Do

We are a small, family owned business conveniently located in Roxbury Township NJ. Here at H 2 THE O we offer fresh, clean, Alkaline and Purified Water to our community and the surrounding areas. We are dedicated to making a healthy and hydrated lifestyle accessible to everyone.

We aim to make every visit to our store:
Pleasant. Providing superior customer service and knowledgeable staff.
Efficient. With an 8 faucet fill station we get you in and out the door quickly.
Affordable. Bottles and premium water at reasonable prices.
Transparant. Our water is officially LAB TESTED AND CERTIFIED and we have the results to prove it! Just ask and we will be happy to share them with you! You can also see the on premises, bottle sterilization, water filtration and filling process from beginning to end at every visit. No more wondering if your bottle AND water are REALLY bacteria, mold, virus and chemical free.

Our water is purified through a 12 step, Reverse Osmosis filtration process which creates refreshing, exceptional quality water; adding natural, essential minerals further enhances the purified water to create our stable, 8+ PH Alkaline water.

Our water bottles are manufactured here in the United States, BGA and BPA free.
